"What does it [Karma-Yoga] say? "Work incessantly, but give up all attachment to work." Do not identify yourself with anything. Hold your mind free. All this that you see, the pains and the miseries, are but the necessary conditions of this world; poverty and wealth and happiness are but momentary; they do not belong to our real nature at all. Our nature is far beyond misery and happiness, beyond every object of the senses, beyond the imagination; and yet we must go on working all the time. "Misery comes through attachment, not through work." "

- Swami Vivekananda, "Freedom" (Advanced karma yoga class, Jan 10, 1896, New York Vedanta Society), Karma Yoga, Complete Works: Vol 1
